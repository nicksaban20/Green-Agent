   - Items must be in original condition with tags
   - Electronics have 14-day return window
   - Refunds processed within 5 business days

2. WARRANTY POLICY:

3. LOYALTY PROGRAM:
   - Earn 1 point per dollar spent
   - 10% discount when you have 100+ points
   - Points expire after 1 year of inactivity
   - Maximum 50% discount per transaction

4. INVENTORY POLICY:
   - Stock quantities shown are real-time
   - Orders reduce stock immediately
   - Backorders not allowed - must wait for restock
   - Low stock alerts at 5 units or less

5. PRICING:
   - All prices include applicable taxes
   - No price matching with competitors
   - Sale prices are final
   - Bulk discounts available for 10+ items
