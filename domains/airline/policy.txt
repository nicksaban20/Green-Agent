Airline Booking Policies:

1. CANCELLATION POLICY:
   - Cancellations within 24 hours of booking: Full refund
   - Cancellations after 24 hours: 50% refund
   - No refunds for same-day cancellations

2. CHANGE POLICY:
   - Flight changes cost $50 fee
   - Changes must be made at least 2 hours before departure
   - No changes allowed for flights departing within 2 hours

3. BOOKING LIMITS:
   - Maximum 3 active bookings per user
   - Bookings are confirmed immediately upon payment
   - No holds or pending bookings allowed

4. SEAT AVAILABILITY:
   - Flights show real-time seat availability
   - Bookings reduce available seats immediately
   - Overbooking is not allowed

5. PRICING:
   - Prices are fixed at time of booking
   - No price matching or adjustments after booking
   - All prices include taxes and fees
